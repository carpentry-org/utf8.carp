UTF8

This module enables you to create and manage UTF-8 encoded strings. Instead of strings, you use UTF-8 objects, and instead of chars, you use Runes. Runes can be stringified normally, and can be asked for their bytes. UTF-8 strings try to mimic the behavior of regular old ASCII strings.
